Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Body Manual AI is a personal health coaching tool. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and how to delete it.

Who this applies to

This app is a private MVP used by its developer and a small number of invited users. It is not a public consumer product.

What data is collected

When you connect your WHOOP account, the app stores the following data on your behalf:

  • WHOOP recovery score, HRV, resting heart rate, respiratory rate, SpO2, and skin temperature
  • Sleep duration, efficiency, performance, and consistency
  • Daily strain and workout activity
  • Your Telegram user ID and first name
  • Your local timezone (used to map WHOOP cycles to calendar days)
  • Optional: daily check-in tags you provide (e.g. "high stress", "late meal")

OAuth tokens (WHOOP access and refresh tokens) are stored to maintain the connection on your behalf.

How your data is used

Your data is used solely to generate your daily coach message and personal health summary. Specifically:

  • A structured summary of your metrics (not your raw history) is sent to OpenAI to generate your morning coach message. No raw time-series data is sent to OpenAI.
  • Your data is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for cross-user analytics.
  • There is no pooling of data across users.

Third-party services

  • WHOOP API — data source. Subject to WHOOP's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI API — used to generate coach message text from pre-computed summaries. Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Telegram — the interface for all interactions. Subject to Telegram's Privacy Policy.

Data retention and deletion

Send /delete to the bot at any time. This permanently and immediately hard-deletes all of your data across every table — recovery metrics, sleep data, coach messages, journal entries, and OAuth tokens. There is no soft-delete, no archive, and no retention period. The deletion is irreversible.

Data security

All data is stored in a private PostgreSQL database. OAuth tokens are stored per-user and isolated from other users' data. Encryption at rest will be applied before the app is shared beyond the developer.

Contact
